CAREER OPPORTUNITIES.

When we are helping a customer deliver on their vision we know it is important to have the best people for the job. Unlike some other consultancies, we won’t put someone in who doesn’t fit the role, which is why when we are unable to fulfil a role internally we work with Associates to ensure we deliver the best result for the customer.

If you’re looking for Associate opportunities then please get in contact through the form below. Following confirming your suitability we will identify any upcoming work that would benefit from your experience. If there is nothing immediately available we will keep a copy of your CV and contact you if an opportunity arises to see if you would still be interested.

We, like every other consultancy, look to hire intelligent individuals. Where we differ is that we look for people who want to go beyond following checklists, we look for those who want to truly understand the customers needs and seek to deliver real value.

HOLIDAY?

All staff get a standard 25 days holiday a year. There is also the option to purchase an additional five days.

TRAINING?

We look to employ individuals who want to push themselves to greater things and Solutions Delivered are supportive of that. To support our staff in developing themselves we not only look at traditional training but also more modern approaches. All staff are able to get access to Pluralsight through Solutions Delivered to take advantage of the courses available there.

BONUS?

We provide performance-linked annual bonuses for staff.
